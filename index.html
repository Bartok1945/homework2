About Me

I am a composer of music and sound designer for the stage, screen, and concert hall. I received my bacehelors and masters degrees in music composition from Shepherd University and Shenandoah Conservatory respectively. For the last decade I have been a company member at the Maryland Ensemble Theatre in Frederick, MD. My work at the theatre includes orginal sound design and orginal musical scores. I am currently an adjunct professor of music and that line of work is terrible. I've decided to learn how to code so I can have more job options.

In addition to my skill set as a musician, I can juggle poorly, and make pretty good pizza. When I grow up I want to be a internationally recognized musician with a succesful crossover career in film/television with an impressive, and diverse, investment portfolio.
